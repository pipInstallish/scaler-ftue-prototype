# Roadmap Video Narration Script

## Version 1
**Target duration:** 75-90 seconds  
**Speaker:** Founder avatar  
**Tone:** calm, premium, confident, reassuring

Welcome to Scaler.

I want to walk you through what your next year can look like from here.

We begin with a meet and greet session, where you get oriented to the journey ahead, understand how the platform works, and get introduced to the support systems around you.

Right after that, you will have your first mentor session. This is where you plan your Scaler journey with an industry expert mentor of your choice, so your path starts with clarity, not confusion.

Then comes your first class. This is where the learning truly begins. You unlock live classes, industry-vetted assignments, live TA support for uninterrupted problem solving, and your twenty-four seven AI companion.

Your first major milestone is the DSA skill certification assessment. As you start clearing certifications, Career Hub opens doors to more than nine hundred hiring partners, and you can begin applying for relevant opportunities early in the journey.

Next comes your SQL certification. This is an important step because it expands the set of roles you become eligible for and strengthens your readiness for practical engineering work.

Once your foundations are in place, you choose a specialization track: Backend, Fullstack, or Data Engineering. This is where your learning starts becoming more aligned to the engineer you want to become.

After that, you move into Scaler's Capstone project, where you apply what you have learned in a more practical, real-world way.

And then comes an important moment. Once your certifications and project depth come together, you become industry ready for a whole new set of opportunities, including newer AI-era roles like AI-augmented engineering, agent engineering, and AI architecture.

From there, you can go deeper through additional AI electives like GenAI and Product Management with AI, depending on the direction you want to grow into.

And even beyond this roadmap, Scaler stays with you as your lifelong learning partner, with continued access to course material as technology keeps evolving.

This journey is structured to help you build real depth, stay on track, and grow into the kind of engineer the future needs.

Let's begin.

## Version 2

**Speaker:** Founder avatar  
**Tone:** tighter, sharper, more product-like

Welcome to Scaler.

Here is what your next year looks like.

Your first class unlocks the full learning experience: live classes, industry-vetted assignments, live TA support, and your twenty-four seven AI companion.

Your first major milestone is the DSA skill certification assessment. As you begin clearing certifications, Career Hub opens doors to more than nine hundred hiring partners.

Then comes your SQL certification, which expands the range of roles you become eligible for.

At the right stage, you choose a specialization track: Backend, Fullstack, or Data Engineering.

You then apply your learning through Scaler's Capstone project and move toward becoming industry ready across both classic software roles and newer AI-era roles.

After that, additional AI electives like GenAI and Product Management with AI open up for you.

And even beyond this first year, Scaler continues as your lifelong learning partner.

This is your roadmap. Let's get started.

## Recommended Cut

# Roadmap Walkthrough Video Script

## Goal
Create a short founder-avatar walkthrough video for the post-onboarding roadmap screen.

- Duration: **60-90 seconds**
- Tone: **premium, clear, reassuring, aspirational**
- Format: **founder avatar narration over the roadmap**
- Purpose: help the learner understand what the next 12 months look like without overwhelming them

## Video Direction

- The avatar should feel like a founder or senior guide, not a salesperson
- The roadmap should scroll or animate vertically as the narration progresses
- Highlight each milestone as it is mentioned
- Use subtle motion, not flashy motion
- Keep the visual style aligned with the dark roadmap screen
- The user should feel: "This journey is structured, supported, and worth committing to."

## Avatar Direction

- Use a calm, confident Indian founder-style avatar
- Delivery should feel conversational, not corporate
- Moderate speaking pace
- Warm but decisive tone
- Maintain eye contact with the camera when possible

## Subtitle Guidelines

- Use sentence case
- Keep subtitles tightly synced to speech
- Avoid showing too much text at once
- Maximum 2 lines at a time

## Music and Audio

- Minimal ambient background music
- No dramatic drops or cinematic trailer style
- Keep the avatar voice clean and primary

## Output Requirements

- Aspect ratio: **16:9**
- Duration: **under 90 seconds**
- Style: **premium product walkthrough**
- Deliverables:
  - one full video
  - one muted autoplay-friendly version with subtitles
